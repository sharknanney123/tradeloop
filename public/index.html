Why trade here instead of selling?

✦
Keep the full value of your cards.

Marketplace fees and shipping eat 15%+ of a cash sale. In a loop, your $40 card buys $40 of someone else's cards.

✦
Trades that were impossible become easy.

In person, a trade needs two people who each want exactly what the other has. The loop engine only needs a chain to exist somewhere in the community.

✦
The hub takes the risk out.

Every package is opened and verified at the hub before anything ships onward. A misgraded card voids the loop — nobody loses their cards.

✦
Built for players and stores.

Players hunt the exact cards their deck needs. Stores dump slow stock and take in whatever the market data says is moving fastest.
